Vampire Soldier




Stats:


HP: 980

MP: 0

PHY STR: 10

PHY DEF: 15

MG STR: 0

MG DEF: 5









Vampire Soldier Skills:

  • Vampiric Bite (Offensive) (Level 0): Deals 200 DMG to the target, and heals 5% of the user’s max HP.
  • Vampiric Slash (Offensive) (Level 5): Deals 500 DMG to the target.
  • Dark Blade (Offensive) (Level 15): Deals 700 DMG to the target, and increases PHY STR by 5 on the first successful hit.
  • Shadow Step (Counter) (Level 20): Engulfs the user in shadows, preventing them from being hit by any non-Light and non-Holy elemental attack (Can only be used twice per battle).
  • Blood Curse (Support) (Level 30): Curses the target, decreasing their PHY STR by 10 upon its usage, and dealing DMG equal to 4% of their max HP each turn. (Can only be used twice per battle, and cannot be used on the same target twice.)
  • Shadow Strike (Counter) (Level 40): A follow up to the skill, “Shadow Step.” If the user successfully manages to use their “Shadow Step” skill, they can then immediately use this skill to attack the target and deal 800 DMG. This counts as a counter skill, and as such does not use up a turn (Can only be used once per battle).
  • Blood Lust (Support) (Level 50): Increases the DMG of all the user’s skill by 50% for three turns, but in exchange, the user cannot heal via the use of any of their skills.
  • Blood Lance (Offensive) (Level 70): Deals 900 DMG, and decreases the target PHY DEF by 12 upon its first successful hit.
  • Blood Thirst (Support) (Level 80): Can only be used when the user’s HP is below 50%. Puts the user in a frenzied state where all of their skills do 65% more DMG. Additionally, the player recovers 10% of their max HP upon each successful hit. However, the user is unable to use items, weapons, or support skills while in this state, and can only launch attacks. This effect lasts for 3 turns (Can only be used once per battle).
  • Summon: Blood Soldier (Support) (Level 90): Summons a Blood Soldier has the inability to take DMG. Additionally, it possesses the skill “Blood Lance”: Deals 900 DMG, and heals the user’s max HP by 20% for each successful hit. This summon remains in battle for 3 turns before then leaving. (Can only be used once per battle).
  • Summon: Blood Legion (Support) (Level 100): Summons 3 Blood Soldiers who each have the inability to take DMG. Additionally, they possess the skill “Blood Lance”: Deals 900 DMG, and heals the user’s max HP by 20% for each successful hit. These summons remain in battle for 3 turns before then leaving. (Can only be used once per battle).
