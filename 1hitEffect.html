1-Hit Effect




"The 1-Hit Effect is a powerful status ailment that drastically reduces a target's max HP to just one HP, but in exchange, all of the target's attacks will deal triple the usual amount of damage. This effect can make the affected target a glass cannon, able to deal out massive damage but also susceptible to being taken down with a single well-placed blow."
