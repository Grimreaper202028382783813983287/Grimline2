Poisoned




"The Poisoned status effect deacreases the target's PHY ATK stat by 30%, and deals poison damage to the target every turn."

Low-Tier: Deals damage equal to 10% of the target's current HP each turn.

Mid-Tier: Deals damage equal to 15% of the target's current HP each turn.

High-Tier: Deals damage equal to 35% of the target's current HP each turn.
