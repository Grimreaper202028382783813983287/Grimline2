Undead Swordsman




Stats:


HP: 750

MP: 0

PHY STR: 20

PHY DEF: 18

MG STR: 0

MG DEF: 18













Vampire Prince Skills:

  • Dark Slash (Offensive) (Level 0): Deal 350 DMG
  • Triple Dark Slash (Offensive) (Level 5): Deals 300 DMG to all foes.
  • Death Beam (Offensive) (Level 15): Deals 200 DMG and decreases a target’s MG DEF by 20%
  • Summon: Skeletal Knight (Support) (Level 15): Summons a Skeletal Knight with the following stats (Can only be used twice per battle):
    • HP: 950
    • Skills:
      • Unholy Slasher: Deals 600 DMG
  • Plague Touch (Support) (Level 25): Decreases the PHY DEF on a single target by 15%
  • Soul Harvest (Support) (Level 35): Absorbs the soul of a single fallen target in order to boost the DMG on all of the user’s attacks by 15% in their next battle (Can only be used outside of battle, and effect cannot stack)
  • Death Grip (Counter) (Level 55): Whenever a target tries to flee from a battle, the user is able to immediately use Dark Slash on them, and if the attack successfully lands, that target will remain in battle and can no longer flee.
  • Last Stand (Counter) (Level 75): Upon the user’s death, they are immediately revived at half of their maximum HP. Additionally, all of their attacks deal double the DMG. However, if they are unable to defeat their opponent in three turns, they will instantly die.
  • Unholy Death Cannon (Counter) (Level 85): Allows the user to either skip anywhere from one to three turns in order to unleash a powerful attack that deals DMG to all foes present. Additionally, the attack decreases the MG DEF and PHY DEF of all foes hit by the attack by 25%:
    • 1 Turn: The attack deals 900 DMG to all foes present
    • 2 Turns: The attacks deals 1200 DMG to all foes present
    • 3 Turns: The attack deals 1300 DMG to all foes present
