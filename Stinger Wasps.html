Stinger Wasps




Stats:


HP: 650

MP: 0

PHY STR: 15

PHY DEF: 10

MG STR: 5

MG DEF: 8













Stinger Wasps Skills:

  • Stinger Strike (Offensive) (Level 0): Deals 300 DMG to a single target, with a 20% chance to apply a Low-Tier Poisoned status effect for 5 turns.
  • Swarm's Wrath (Offensive) (Level 5): Unleashes a barrage of stings, dealing 400 DMG to all foes present. If the target is affected by the Poisoned status effect, the damage increases to 500 DMG and the Poisoned status effect tier upgrades to Mid-Tier.
  • Venomous Touch (Support) (Level 10): Enhances the damage of a single attack by 35%. If the attack hits, the target is also afflicted with a Low-Tier Poisoned status effect, for 5 turns. (Can only be used twice per battle)
  • Lethal Injection (Support) (Level 25): The target loses 10% of their max HP and is afflicted with a Mid-Tier Poisoned status effect. The Wasp then absorbs the lost HP, healing themselves by the same amount. (Can only be used twice per battle)
  • Hivemind's Hunger (Offensive) (Level 35): Deals 800 DMG to all foes present and increases the PHY ATK of all nearby Stinger Wasps by 15% for 3 turns. (The effect of this skill can only be applied twice per battle)
  • Predator's Instincts (Counter) (Level 45): When hit by an attack that would normally kill them, the Stinger Wasp can counter with a devastating sting, dealing DMG equal to 20% of the attacker's max HP and negating the attack. (Can only be used once per battle)
  • Stunning Venom (Counter) (Level 55): Counters an attack by unleashing a potent venom, dealing 300 DMG and applying a Low-Tier Stun status effect to the target, preventing them from acting for one turn. (Attack still hits the user) (Can only be used twice per battle)
  • Unyielding Resilience (Support) (Level 75): Allows the user to skip one of their turns. On their next turn, the skill restores the user's HP to full and grants them the Brave status effect, as well as a High-Tier Defended status effect for 3 turns. (Can only be used twice per battle)
  • Queen's Command (Support) (Level 95): Increases the PHY ATK and PHY DEF of the user to their maximum values for the rest of the battle. Additionally, the Queen's Command grants all present insectoids immunity to the Instant Death status effect and a High-Tier Defended status effect for 3 turns. (Can only be used once per battle)
