Beastmen















Beastmen Racial Traits:

  • Bestial Rage (Passive) - When a Beastman's HP falls below 30%, they enter a state of Bestial Rage, granting them a 20% boost to PHY ATK and PHY DEF for one turn. This trait cannot occur more than once per battle.
  • Primal Instincts (Passive) - Beastmen have a 20% chance to act twice in a single turn if their HP is below 50%. This trait cannot occur more than once per battle.
  • Feral Healing (Passive) - At the start of each turn, if a Beastman has a positive status effect, they recover 5% of their maximum HP.
  • Dark Resistance (Passive): Beastmen have a 20% resistance to Dark element attacks.
  • Fire Resistance (Passive): Beastmen have a 15% resistance to Fire element attacks.
  • Water Vulnerability (Passive): Beastmen have a 15% vulnerability to Water element attacks.
  • Ice Vulnerability (Passive): Beastmen have a 15% vulnerability to ice element attacks.
