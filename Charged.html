Charged




"The Charged status effect causes the user to skip a certain number of turns while focusing their energy and concentration in order to unleash a more powerful attack."

Low-Tier: The user skips one turn, and their next attack deals 50% more damage.

Mid-Tier: The user skips two turns, and their next attack deals double the damage.

High-Tier: The user skips three turns, and their next attack deals triple the damage.
