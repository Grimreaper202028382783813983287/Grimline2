Mushroom Mound




Stats:


HP: 920

MP: 0

PHY STR: 14

PHY DEF: 22

MG STR: 0

MG DEF: 7













Mushroom Mound Skills:

  • Sporal Burst (Offensive) (Level 0) - Hurls a barrage of infectious spores at a single target, dealing 300 DMG. Each spore has a 25% chance to apply a Low-Tier Poisoned status effect for 3 turns, which deals 50 DMG per turn.
  • Mushroom Maze (Support) (Level 5) - Summons a labyrinth of mushrooms around the user, granting them a 20% increase to PHY DEF and a 15% increase to MAG DEF for 4 turns. Enemies that attack the Mushroom Mound while in the maze take 75 DMG per attack and have a 20% chance to suffer a Low-Tier Poisoned status effect for 2 turns (Can only be used twice per battle).
  • Toxic Spore Cloud (Offensive) (Level 15) - Releases a noxious cloud of spores in a cone formation, dealing 400 DMG to all enemies hit and having a 40% chance to apply a Low-Tier Poisoned status effect for 5 turns. Enemies hit by the attack take a 25% increase in damage from the Mushroom Mound's next attack (Can only be used twice per battle).
  • Fungal Armor (Support) (Level 20) - Reinforces the Mushroom Mound's natural armor with a layer of fungi, increasing its PHY DEF by 40% for 5 turns. While active, the Mushroom Mound gains a 30% boost to damage dealt to enemies with the Poisoned status effect (Can only be used once per battle).
  • Entangling Mycelium (Offensive) (Level 30) - Summons a mass of twisting, fungal tendrils to ensnare a single target, dealing 450 DMG and rooting them in place for 3 turns (Turn skip effect only works once per battle). The rooted target takes 100 DMG per turn from the tendrils and has a 30% chance to suffer a Low-Tier Poisoned status effect for 2 turns.
  • Mushroom Swarm (Offensive) (Level 40) - Summons a swarm of infected mushrooms to attack all enemies in a large area, dealing 600 DMG. Enemies hit by the swarm take 100 DMG per turn and have a 30% chance to suffer a Mid-Tier Poisoned and Mid-Tier Blind status effect for 5 turns (DMG per turn cannot stack).
  • Sporal Detonation (Offensive) (Level 50) - Hurls a massive spore at a single target, dealing 750 DMG. The spore has a 60% chance to apply the Mid-Tier Poisoned status effect for 5 turns and a 20% chance to apply a Mid-Tier Stunned status effect (Can only be used once per battle).
  • Fungal Frenzy (Support) (Level 60) - Enrages the Mushroom Mound, increasing its PHY ATK and MAG ATK by 35% for 4 turns. While enraged, the Mushroom Mound gains a 15% boost to damage dealt to enemies with the Poisoned status effect (Can only be used once per battle).
  • Fungal Fury (Offensive) (Level 70) - The Mushroom Mound's fungal nature surges with a virulent rage, amplifying the potency of its next attack. This skill boosts the damage dealt by the attack by 50%, while also giving the target a High-Tier Poisoned status effect for 3 turns. Enemies afflicted by the effect will have a 30% chance to receive the Random status effect (Can only be used once per battle).
  • Mushroom Shield (Support) (Level 80) - Creates a shield of mushrooms around the Mushroom Mound, increasing its MG DEF by 50% for 5 turns. While active, the Mushroom Mound gains a 15% boost to damage dealt to enemies that attack it. Enemies that attack the shield take 300 DMG and have a 20% chance to suffer a High-Tier Poisoned status effect for 3 turns (Can only be used once per battle).
  • Fungal Leviathan (Support) (Level 90) - Transforms the Mushroom Mound into a colossal, mutated abomination for 5 turns. In this form, the Mushroom Mound gains a 40% boost to all stats and a 25% boost to damage dealt. Enemies that attack the Fungal Leviathan take 650 DMG and suffer a High-Tier Poisoned status effect for 5 turns (Can only be used once per battle).
  • Spore Storm Surge (Offensive) (Level 100) - The Mushroom Mound unleashes a cataclysmic eruption of infectious spores, dealing 1200 DMG to all enemies caught in the blast. For the duration of the skill, enemies suffer the Cursed status effect, causing them to take a 30% boost to damage from all sources for 10 turns (Can only be used once per battle).
