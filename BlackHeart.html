Black Heart




"Black Heart is a status effect that makes it so that each turn, the target takes damage equal to 5% of their maximum HP, and their stats are reduced by 10. To escape the Black Heart's effects, the only option is to defeat all foes present in battle, as there is no other way to remove this relentless status effect."
