Spirits















Spirits Racial Traits:

  • Disease Immunity
  • Instant Death Immunity
  • Doubled Damage From Fire
  • Doubled Damage From Magic Attacks
  • Physical Attack Immunity
  • Inability to equip armor or weapons
  • Hunger Immunity
  • Life Drain Immunity
  • Holy and Light Vulnerability: All damage taken from attacks related to the Holy and Light element are increased by 45%.
  • Darkness Resistance: All damage taken from attacks related to the Dark element are decreased by 45%
