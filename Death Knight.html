Death Knight




Stats:


HP: 550

MP: 0

PHY STR: 17

PHY DEF: 15

MG STR: 0

MG DEF: 8













Death Knight Skills:

  • Unholy Slash (Offensive) (Level 0): Deals 300 DMG
  • Dark Pulse (Offensive) (Level 5): Deals 400 DMG to all foes present
  • Necrotic Touch (Support) (Level 10): Increases the DMG of a single attack by 35% (Can only be used twice per battle)
  • Life Leech (Support) (Level 25): When the user attacks a target, the target will additionally lose 7% of their max HP, which the user in turn will absorb, causing them to heal by that same amount (Can only be used three times per battle).
  • Aura of Darkness (Offensive) (Level 35): Deals 800 DMG and decreases the PHY ATK of all foes present by 15%. (The effect of this skill can only be applied twice per battle).
  • Soul Taker (Counter) (Level 45): Deals DMG equal to 10% of a target’s max HP, which the user themself then heals from, and prevents them from fleeing a battle (Can only be used once per battle).
  • Chains of Torment (Counter) (Level 55): Negates an attack and decreases the target’s PHY DEF by 25% (Can only be used twice per battle)
  • Final Stance (Counter) (Level 65): Whenever the user is hit by an attack that would normally kill them, they are instead left alive at 1 HP. Additionally, the user is given the 1-hit status effect (Can only be used once per battle).
  • Dark Ritual (Support) (Level 75): Use first skips one of their turns. Then, the skill restores the user’s HP back to full, and gives them the Brave status effect, as well as a Mid-Tier Defended status effect for 3 turns.
  • Lord of Death (Support) (Level 85): Increases PHY ATK and PHY DEF to max for the rest of the battle, and makes them immune to any and all negative status effects for 3 turns (Can only be used once per battle).
