Rules


1. In the game, each player is granted one turn per rotation, meaning they must wait for all players to take their turn before receiving theirs again. However, this does not apply in battles, where players will take turns with their enemy until the battle is over. During a turn, players can perform up to three actions: attack using a skill, spell, or weapon, use a support skill to aid themselves or an ally, and utilize as many counter-skills or counter-spells as they wish all in one turn.


2. Players have the option to use up their turns in order to perform various actions such as buying items at the in-game shop and attempting to conquer challenging dungeons.


3. In Grimline, the maximum stats for Magical Defense (MG DEF), Physical Defense (PHY DEF), Physical Strength (PHY STR) and Magic Strength (MG STR) are both 200. The maximum value for Magic Points (MP) cannot exceed 2,000.


4. In Grimline, HP and MP stats of a player do not automatically restore to full after each battle. The HP and MP stats remain consistent throughout the game. However, upon leveling up, HP and MP will be restored back to full.


5. The Physical Strength (PHY STR) and Mana Strength (MG STR) stat in Grimline exclusively influence the damage output of weapons and offensive skills and spells. They have no impact on the effectiveness of support skills and do not affect any damage over time (DOT) effects.





Death Penalties


1st Death: In Grimline, players face no penalties upon their first death. No XP, currency, or items are lost, and the player is simply reset to the last location they were in previously.


2nd Death: Upon a player's second death in Grimline, all stats except for HP and MP are reduced by a flat value of 15 for a period of three battles.


3rd Death: If a player suffers a third death in Grimline, they will receive the same penalty as the second death, losing 15 from all stats except HP and MP. In addition, the player will lose all consumable items in their inventory. If the player dies multiple times after the third death, the same effects will continue to apply
