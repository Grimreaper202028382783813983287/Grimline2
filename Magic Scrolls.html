Magic Scrolls






List of Magic Scrolls:

  • Fireball Scroll - Deals 400 Fire elemental damage to a single target and has a 30% chance to apply a Low-Tier Burned status effect for 3 turns.
  • Frostbolt Scroll - Deals 400 Ice elemental damage to a single target and has a 30% chance to apply the Frozen status effect to the target for 2 turns.
  • Thunderbolt Scroll - Deals 400 Lightning elemental damage to a single target and has a 40% chance to apply the Stunned status effect to the target, preventing them from acting for 1 turn.
  • Entangle Scroll - Summons a mass of thorny vines to ensnare a single target, dealing 200 Nature elemental damage per turn for 3 turns.
  • Heal Scroll - Restores 600 HP to a single target and removes one random negative status effect.
  • Mighty Heal Scroll - Restores 1200 HP to a single target and removes all negative status effects.
  • Dispel Scroll - Removes all positive and negative status effects from a single target and grants them a 20% boost to all stats for 1 turn.
  • Power Scroll - Grants a 50% boost to PHY ATK and MAG ATK to a single target for 1 turn.
  • Mystic Scroll - Grants a 30% boost to all stats and a 20% boost to damage dealt to a single target for 1 turn.
  • Annihilation Scroll - Deals 800 Chaos elemental damage to all enemies and has a 50% chance to apply the Corrupted status effect for 5 turns.
  • Sacrifice Scroll - Instantly defeats the user if their HP is below 75% at the start of their turn, but deals 2000 Chaos elemental damage to all enemies. Also deals DMG to the user equal to 50% of their max HP.
  • Time Scroll - Allows the user to act twice in the next turn, with the second action dealing 50% reduced damage should it be an attack.
