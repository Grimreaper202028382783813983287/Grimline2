Uknowables







Stats:


HP: 796

MP: 0

PHY STR: 21

PHY DEF: 3

MG STR: 17

MG DEF: 9

Alignement: Neutral












Uknowables Racial Traits:

  • Stat Blocker (Passive): User’s stats (besides HP and MP) will remain the same throughout the entire game.
  • DEF Bypasser (Passive): All attacks ignore PHY DEF and MG DEF.
  • Armor Breaker (Passive): User cannot wield weapons or armor of any kind.
  • Schrödinger's Fate (Passive): Once per battle, when the Unknowable is defeated, there is a 50% chance that they will resurrect with 10% of their maximum HP and a random positive status effect. If they do not resurrect, the enemies that defeated them will each take 500 DMG.
  • Causality Fragility (Passive): The Unknowable takes a 20% damage increase from attacks that have a guaranteed effect, such as the Poisoned or Stunned status effect.
  • Wave-Function Collapse (Passive): If the Unknowable uses the same skill twice in a row, there is a 50% chance that the skill will fail and the Unknowable will suffer a random negative status effect. The tier of the negative status effect increases with each consecutive use of the same skill.


Uknowable Skills:

  • Quantum Strike (Offensive) (Level 0): Deals randomized damage between 1600% and 5500% of their PHY ATK. There is a 25% chance the attack will also inflict a random status effect.
  • Fractured Form (Support) (Level 5): The Unknowable splits into 2 duplicate copies of themselves, each with 30% of the original's stats. These duplicates will attack random enemies and then vanish after 3 turns. (Can only be used once per battle).
  • Probability Bomb (Offensive) (Level 10): The Unknowable throws a bomb that explodes after a random delay (between 1-3 turns). The explosion deals randomized damage between 400-800 DMG to all enemies present.
  • Schrödinger's Rift (Counter) (Level 20): Opens a rift to a parallel dimension, pulling a random number of enemies (between 1-5) into it. Enemies trapped in the rift take 600 DMG per turn and have a 42% chance to be afflicted by a random negative status effect. The rift lasts for a random duration (between 2-5 turns) (Can only be used twice per battle).
  • Superposition Field (Support) (Level 45): The Unknowable creates a field around themselves that deals 751 DMG to all enemies present each turn. The damage element type and the number of enemies affected are randomized each turn. The field lasts for a random duration (between 3-7 turns) (Can only be used twice per battle).
  • Entangled Fates (Support) (Level 65): The Unknowable selects an enemy and an ally. If the ally deals damage to the enemy, the enemy will take an additional 300% of the damage dealt. However, if the enemy deals damage to the ally, the Unknowable will take the same amount of damage. This effect lasts for 3 turns or until the enemy is defeated. (Can only be used once per battle).
  • Oblivion's Embrace (Support) (Level 78): The Unknowable then vanishes, causing them to temporarily be immune to harm (randomly chosen between 1-3 turns). Upon reappearing, the Unknowable gains a 50% boost to all stats and a 100% boost to PHY ATK.
