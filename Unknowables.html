Uknowables







Stats:


HP: 300

PHY STR: 10

PHY DEF: 10

MG STR: 10

MG DEF: 10












Uknowables Racial Traits:

  • Stat Blocker (Passive): User’s stats (besides HP and MP) will remain the same throughout the entire game.
  • Fractured Reality (Passive): When an enemy attacks the Unknowable, there is a 20% chance that the attack will hit a duplicate of the Unknowable instead of the original. The duplicate will take the full damage and then vanish.
  • Heisenberg's Uncertainty (Passive): The Unknowable's attacks have a 25% chance to ignore the target's PHY and MG DEF and deal true damage. Additionally, the damage dealt is randomized between 50% and 150% of the Unknowable's PHY ATK.
  • Schrödinger's Fate (Passive): Once per battle, when the Unknowable is defeated, there is a 50% chance that they will resurrect with 10% of their maximum HP and a random positive status effect. If they do not resurrect, the enemies that defeated them will each take 500 DMG.
  • Causality Fragility (Passive): The Unknowable takes a 20% damage increase from attacks that have a guaranteed effect, such as the Poisoned or Stunned status effect.
  • Wave-Function Collapse (Passive): If the Unknowable uses the same skill twice in a row, there is a 50% chance that the skill will fail and the Unknowable will suffer a random negative status effect. The tier of the negative status effect increases with each consecutive use of the same skill.
  • Superposition (Passive): The Unknowable has a 30% resistance to all magical damage, as their physical form exists in multiple states simultaneously.


Uknowable Skills:

  • Quantum Strike (Offensive) (Level 0): The Unknowable's next attack deals randomized damage between 1000% and 9000% of their PHY ATK. There is a 25% chance the attack will also inflict a random status effect.
  • Fractured Form (Support) (Level 5): The Unknowable splits into 1-3 duplicate copies of themselves, each with 30% of the original's stats. These duplicates will attack random enemies and then vanish after 2 turns. The original Unknowable gains a 15% damage reduction while the duplicates remain present in battle. (Can only be used once per battle)
  • Probability Bomb (Offensive) (Level 10): The Unknowable throws a bomb that explodes after a random delay (between 1-3 turns). The explosion deals randomized damage between 400-800 DMG to all enemies present.
  • Schrödinger's Rift (Support) (Level 20): Opens a rift to a parallel dimension, pulling a random number of enemies (between 1-5) into it. Enemies trapped in the rift take 200 DMG per turn and have a 30% chance to be afflicted by a random negative status effect. The rift lasts for a random duration (between 2-5 turns) (Can only be used twice per battle).
  • Wave-Function Collapse Strike (Offensive) (Level 30): The Unknowable channels their chaotic energy into a single, powerful attack that deals damage equal to 1000% of their PHY ATK. However, there is a 50% chance the attack will backfire, dealing the same damage to the Unknowable instead. There is also a 25% chance the attack will cause a random environmental hazard, such as a localized earthquake or gravitational anomaly.
  • Superposition Field (Support) (Level 45): The Unknowable creates a field around themselves that deals 200 DMG to all enemies present each turn. The damage element type and the number of enemies affected are randomized each turn. The field lasts for a random duration (between 3-7 turns) (Can only be used twice per battle).
  • Entangled Fates (Support) (Level 75): The Unknowable selects an enemy and an ally. If the ally deals damage to the enemy, the enemy will take an additional 300% of the damage dealt. However, if the enemy deals damage to the ally, the Unknowable will take the same amount of damage. This effect lasts for 3 turns or until the enemy is defeated. (Can only be used once per battle).
  • Parallel Lives (Support) (Level 90): The Unknowable summons a random number of shadowy doubles of themselves (between 1-5) that attack random enemies for 3 turns. The doubles have the same skills as the user (Besides “Fractured Form”) and have a 25% chance to inflict a random status effect. The original Unknowable gains a 20% damage reduction while the doubles persist (Can only be used once per battle).
  • Oblivion's Embrace (Offensive) (Level 100): Deals 1000 DMG to all enemies. The Unknowable then vanishes, causing them to temporarily be immune to harm (between 1-3 turns). Upon reappearing, the Unknowable gains a 50% boost to all stats and a 100% boost to PHY ATK.
