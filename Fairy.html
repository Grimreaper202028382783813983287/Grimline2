Fairy







Stats:


HP: 420

MP: 25

PHY STR: 0

PHY DEF: 0

MG STR: 20

MG DEF: 15












Fairy Racial Traits:

  • Enchanted Metabolism (Passive): Fairies naturally regenerate MP over time, recovering 2% of their maximum MP every turn in battle.
  • Charm Resistance (Passive): Fairies can attack those with the Loveable status effect.
  • Faerie Dust (Passive): Once per battle, when a Fairy's HP falls below 20%, they can leave a trail of faerie dust that damages enemies for 100 DMG and applies a random status effect.
  • Pixie's Wrath (Passive): If an enemy kills a Fairy, they have a 50% chance to receive a permanent Cursed status effect.
  • Nature's Blessing (Passive): Fairies have a 25% resistance to Nature element attacks.
  • Pixie's Veil (Passive): Fairies have a 15% resistance to Light element attacks.
  • Iron Weakness (Passive): Fairies take a 30% damage increase from Iron weapons or attacks infused with Iron.


Fairy Spells:

  • Pixie Blast (Offensive) (Level 0): Deals 250 Magical DMG to a single target. (Costs 5 MP)
  • Pixie's Enchantment (Support) (Level 5): Charms a single target, forcing them to attack their nearest enemy randomly for 3 turns. (Costs 10 MP)
  • Dust Cloud (Support) (Level 15): Creates a cloud of faerie dust around a target, dealing 100 Magical DMG and applying a random positive status effect to nearby allies each turn for 5 turns. (Costs 20 MP)
  • Mischievous Leap (Support) (Level 25): The Fairy leaps to a random location, leaving behind a duplicate that takes 50% of any damage dealt to it for 2 turns. (Costs 15 MP)
