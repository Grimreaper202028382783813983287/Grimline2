Fairy







Stats:


HP: 680

MP: 30

PHY STR: 0

PHY DEF: 0

MG STR: 20

MG DEF: 25

Alignement: Good












Fairy Racial Traits:

  • Enchanted Metabolism (Passive): Fairies naturally regenerate MP over time, recovering 5% of their maximum MP every turn in battle.
  • Charm Immunity (Passive): Fairies can attack those with the Loveable status effect.
  • Faerie Dust (Passive): Once per battle, when a Fairy's HP falls below 20%, they can leave a trail of faerie dust that damages all enemies for 400 DMG and applies a random status effect.
  • Pixie's Wrath (Passive): If an enemy kills a Fairy, they have a 50% chance to receive a permanent Cursed status effect.
  • Nature's Blessing (Passive): Fairies have a 25% resistance to Nature element attacks.
  • Pixie's Veil (Passive): Fairies have a 15% resistance to Light element attacks.
  • Iron Weakness (Passive): Fairies take a 30% damage increase from Iron weapons or attacks infused with Iron.


Fairy Spells:

  • Pixie Blast (Offensive) (Level 0): Deals 250 DMG. (Costs 5 MP)
  • Pixie's Enchantment (Support) (Level 5): Forces a single target to attack their nearest enemy randomly for 2 turns. (Only works on Enemies and Players that are level 10 and below) (Costs 10 MP)
  • Dust Cloud (Counter) (Level 15): Creates a cloud of faerie dust around a target, dealing 100 DMG and applying a random positive status effect to nearby allies each turn for 5 turns. (Costs 20 MP)
  • Mischievous Leap (Support) (Level 25): Creates a duplicate that takes 50% of any damage dealt to the user for 2 turns. (Costs 15 MP)
  • Fairy Fire (Offensive) (Level 30): Deals 400 DMG to a single target and applies the Burning status effect for 3 turns. (Costs 25 MP)
  • Whispering Winds (Support) (Level 35): Increases the user’s MG STR by 30% for 3 turns. (Costs 30 MP)
  • Pixie's Hex (Offensive) (Level 40): Causes a target to take 20% increased damage for 5 turns. (Costs 35 MP)
  • Dreamscape (Support) (Level 50): Makes the user invisible for 3 turns. (Costs 40 MP)
  • Faerie's Fury (Offensive) (Level 60): Deals 600 Magical DMG to all enemies, with 20% increased damage to enemies with the Cursed status effect. (Costs 45 MP)
  • Eternal Spring (Support) (Level 70): Removes all negative status effects from the user and restores 60% of their maximum HP. (Costs 50 MP)
  • Forests' Wrath (Support) (Level 75): Summons a barrage of enchanted arrows that deal 500 DMG to all enemies for 5 turns. (Costs 55 MP)
  • Pixie's Bane (Offensive) (Level 85): Deals 800 DMG to a single target and has a 50% chance to instantly kill them if their current HP is below 9% of their max HP. (Doesn’t work on Bosses) (Costs 60 MP)
  • Midsummer Night's Dream (Support) (Level 90): Gives all targets a Mid-Tier Stunned status effect for 3 turns, with a 25% chance to also apply the Cursed status effect. (Costs 50% of the user’s max MP)
  • Autumn's Embrace (Offensive) (Level 95): Deals 950 DMG to all enemies, with an additional 300 extra damage to enemies with the Cursed, Loveable, or Black Heart status effect. (Costs 80 MP)
  • Pixie's Wrath (Ultimate) (Level 100): Deals 1500 DMG to all enemies and applies the Cursed status effect for 10 turns. (Costs all remaining MP)
