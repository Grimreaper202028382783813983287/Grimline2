Burrowing Mantes




Stats:


HP: 700

MP: 0

PHY STR: 12

PHY DEF: 15

MG STR: 5

MG DEF: 10













Burrowing Mantes Skills:

  • Tunnel Strike (Offensive) (Level 0): Deals 350 DMG to a single target.
  • Subterranean Surge (Offensive) (Level 5): The user burrows underground and emerges from beneath the target, dealing 450 DMG.
  • Sand Spray (Support) (Level 10): Deals 150 DMG and reduces the target’s PHY DEF by 10%.
  • Burrow's Grasp (Support) (Level 20): The user burrows underground and emerges, grappling a single target and pinning them down. The target is trapped and takes 300 additional DMG per turn for 3 turns, and their PHY DEF is decreased by 20%. (Can only be used three times per battle)
  • Sandstorm's Wrath (Offensive) (Level 35): The user summons a localized sandstorm around themselves, dealing 600 DMG to all enemies within the area and reducing their PHY ATK by 15%. The user gains a 15% PHY ATK bonus for the duration of the sandstorm. (Can only be used twice per battle)
  • Funnel Trap (Support) (Level 50): The user digs a deep hole and hides inside, becoming invisible for 3 turns and gaining a 30% damage reduction. (Can only be used once per battle)
  • Sandstorm's Fury (Offensive) (Level 60): The user channels the power of the sandstorm, dealing 700 DMG to all enemies and reducing their PHY ATK by 20%. The user also gains a 20% PHY ATK increase as well. (Can only be used twice per battle)
  • Entomb (Support) (Level 65): The user entombs a single target in a cocoon of sand, trapping them and preventing them from acting for 2 turns. The target takes 250 DMG per turn while entombed. (Can only be used once per battle)
  • Sandstorm's Wrath (Offensive) (Level 70): The user unleashes a massive sandstorm, dealing 900 DMG to all enemies on the battlefield and reducing their PHY DEF by 25%. The user also gains a 25% PHY ATK increase. (Can only be used twice per battle)
  • Sandstorm's Grasp (Support) (Level 75): The user summons a wall of whirling sand to block a targeted path, granting allies a 20% damage reduction and a 15% increase in PHY ATK (Can only be used three times per battle).
  • Sandstorm's Onslaught (Offensive) (Level 85): Deals 1000 DMG to a single target (Can only be used three times per battle).
  • Burrowing Rage (Support) (Level 90): The user gains a 50% increase in PHY ATK and their attacks deal a 25% damage to enemies whose PHY DEF is lower than 20. Additionally, their attacks have a 50% chance to decrease the target’s PHY DEF by 50%. (Can only be used twice per battle)
  • Burrowing Dominion (Support) (Level 100): All of the user’s allies gain a 30% increase in PHY ATK and a 20% damage reduction. Additionally, if an ally defeats an enemy while under the effects of this skill, they gain a 50% PHY ATK bonus for the next turn. (Can only be used once per battle)
