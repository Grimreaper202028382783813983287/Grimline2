Weather



Weather: Weather is a mechanic in Grimline that takes the form of a phenomenon that affects everyone present (including enemies).









List of Weather Types:







Clear: Default weather. No special effects occur.








Rainy: Every turn, everyone present will receive the Wet status effect.








Blizzard: Every turn, everyone present will receive the Frozen status effect.








Hot Ash: Every turn, everyone present will receive the Burning status effect.









Sunny: Can be used to cancel out Rainy weather, and each turn gives everyone present the Morale status effect.








Acid Rain: Every turn, everyone present will receive the Poisoned status effect.







Hail: Every turn, everyone present will receive the Chilled status effect.







Cherry Blossom: Every 3 turns, everyone present will receive the Haste status effect.








Breezy: Every 3 turns, everyone present will receive the Blessed status effect.





Sandstorm: Every turn, everyone present will receive the Dry status effect.






Low-Gravity: Every turn, everyone present will receive the Light status effect.








High-Gravity: Every turn, everyone present will receive the Heavy status effect.






Fog: Every 4 turns, everyone present will receive the Shrouded status effect.






Thunder Storm: Every 3 turns, 3 random people present in the battle will be struck by lightning, dealing damage equal to 20% of their current HP, and inflicting the Stunned status effect on them.







Sacred Land: Every 3 turns, 3 random people present in the battle will either receive the Good Luck or Blessed status effect (50% chance of getting either effect).






Radiation: At the start of the battle, the PHY DEF and MG DEF of everyone present in the battle will be decreased by 25%.
