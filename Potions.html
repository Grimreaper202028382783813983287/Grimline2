Potions










List of Healing Potions:

  • Minor Healing Potion - Restores 300 HP.
  • Lesser Healing Potion - Restores 600 HP.
  • Healing Potion - Restores 1000 HP.
  • Greater Healing Potion - Restores 2000 HP.
  • Superior Healing Potion - Restores 3000 HP and removes one random negative status effect.
  • Ultimate Healing Elixir - Fully restores all lost HP and removes all negative status effects.












List of Mana Potions:

  • Minor Mana Potion - Restores 100 MP.
  • Lesser Mana Potion - Restores 400 MP.
  • Mana Potion - Restores 600 MP.
  • Greater Mana Potion - Restores 1000 MP.
  • Superior Mana Potion - Restores 1500 MP and grants a 15% boost to MG ATK.
  • Ultimate Mana Elixir - Fully restores all lost MP and grants a 30% boost to MG ATK and MG DEF.












List of Support Potions:

  • Stamina Drops - Grants a 30% boost to PHY ATK and PHY DEF.
  • Vigor Tonic - Grants a 30% boost to PHY ATK and a 15% boost to PHY DEF.
  • Elixir of Vitality - Grants a 50% boost to PHY ATK and PHY DEF and removes one random negative status effect.
  • Hero's Tonic - Grants a 50% boost to PHY ATK and MG ATK.
  • Philosopher's Stone - Grants a 30% boost to all stats and removes all negative status effects.












List of Special Potions:

  • Elixir of Life - Grants immunity to the next single-target attack and removes all negative status effects.
  • Potion of Heroism - Allows the user to act twice in the next turn, with the second action dealing 50% reduced damage.
  • Tears of the Goddess - Grants a 20% boost to all stats and a 30% boost to damage dealt to enemies with positive status effects for 5 turns.
  • Nectar of the Gods - Allows the user to revive once if defeated, with 50% of their maximum HP and MP restored.












List of Flasks:

  • Ember Flask - Grants 50% resistance to Fire elemental damage and immunity to the Burned status effect for one turn.
  • Frost Flask - Grants 50% resistance to Ice and Water elemental damage and immunity to the Frozen status effect for one turn.
  • Storm Flask - Grants 50% resistance to Lightning and Wind elemental damage and immunity to the Stunned status effect for one turn.
  • Flora Flask - Grants 50% resistance to Nature and Earth elemental damage and immunity to Poison and Cursed status effects for one turn.
  • Lumina Flask - Grants 50% resistance to Light elemental damage and immunity to the Blind status effect for one turn.
  • Umbra Flask - Grants 50% resistance to Darkness elemental damage and immunity to the Instant Death and Deletion status effects for one turn.
  • Mystic Flask - Grants 30% resistance to all elemental damages and immunity to the next single-target attack for one turn.
  • Purge Flask - Removes all positive and negative status effects from the user and grants a 20% boost to all stats for one turn.
  • Phantom Flask - Allows the user to act twice in the next turn, with the second action dealing 50% reduced damage for one turn.
  • Astral Flask - Grants a 30% boost to damage dealt to enemies with negative status effects and a 20% boost to damage taken by enemies with positive status effects for one turn.
  • Noxious Flask - Deals 300 DMG to all enemies and applies a random negative status effect for one turn. The user cannot be targeted by enemies for one turn after using this flask.
  • Fatal Flask - Instantly defeats the user if their HP is below 50% at the start of their turn, but grants a 50% boost to all stats and damage dealt for that turn if used.
