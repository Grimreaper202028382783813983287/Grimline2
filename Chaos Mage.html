Chaos Mage




Stats:


HP: 670

MP: 30

PHY STR: 0

PHY DEF: 0

MG STR: 30

MG DEF: 12

Alignment: Evil













Chaos Meter (Level 50): The Chaos Meter naturally increases by 1 stage every 2 turns. The Chaos Meter automatically resets after every battle, or whenever the user dies.

  • Stage 1: User’s attacks all do 10% more DMG, and each of the user’s attacks decrease the target’s MG DEF by 10.
  • Stage 2: User’s attacks all do 30% more DMG, and each of the user’s attacks decrease the target’s MG DEF by 20.
  • Stage 3: User’s attacks all do 50% more DMG, and each of the user’s attacks decrease the target’s MG DEF by 35. All of the user’s attacks ignore the MG DEF stat.


Chaos Mage Skills:

  • Chaos Bolt (Offensive) (Level 0): Deals 550 DMG to a single target. (Costs 15 MP)
  • Hex Blast (Offensive) (Level 5): Deals 700 DMG to a single target. (Costs 35 MP)
  • Pandemonium (Support) (Level 20): Induces temporary insanity in a target, causing them to attack random allies for 3 turns. (Costs 55 MP)
  • Dissonance (Offensive) (Level 30): Deals 850 DMG and applies the BlackHeart status effect for 2 turns. (Costs 130 MP)
  • Cacophony (Support) (Level 35): Boosts the user's MG STR by 40% for 3 turns. (Costs 165 MP)
  • Cataclysm (Offensive) (Level 40): Deals 900 DMG to all enemies. (Costs 270 MP)
  • Disintegration (Counter) (Level 50): Destroys a consumable item before it is used, and decreases the target’s PHY DEF by 20%. (Costs 280 MP)
  • Anarchy's Grasp (Support) (Level 60): Binds a target, preventing them from acting and dealing 600 DMG each turn for 2 turns. (Costs 290 MP)
  • Chaos Storm (Support) (Level 75): Summons a storm of chaotic energy that deals 700 DMG to all enemies for 3 turns. (Costs 300 MP)
  • Entropy's Embrace (Offensive) (Level 85): Deals 1200 DMG to a single target and has a 40% chance to instantly kill them if they are below 12% HP. (Does not work on Bosses) (Costs 410 MP)
  • Entropy's Embrace (Offensive) (Level 85): Deals 1200 Magical DMG to a single target and has a 60% chance to instantly kill them if they are below 30% HP. (Costs 410 MP)
  • Anarchy's Wrath (Offensive) (Level 95): Deals 1800 DMG to all enemies and applies the Cursed status effect for 15 turns. (Costs all remaining MP)
