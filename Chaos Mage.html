Chaos Mage




Stats:


HP: 620

MP: 20

PHY STR: 0

PHY DEF: 0

MG STR: 25

MG DEF: 10













Chaos Mage Skills:

  • Chaos Bolt (Offensive) (Level 0): Unleashes a bolt of chaotic energy dealing 350 Magical DMG to a single target. (Costs 15 MP)
  • Hex Blast (Offensive) (Level 5): Fragments a target with hex-infused shards, dealing 200 Magical DMG to the target and nearby enemies. (Costs 35 MP)
  • Pandemonium (Support) (Level 15): Induces temporary insanity in a target, causing them to attack random allies for 3 turns. (Costs 55 MP)
  • Anarchy's Leap (Support) (Level 25): The Chaos mage teleports to a random location, leaving behind a shadow clone that takes 75% damage for 2 turns. (Costs 70 MP)
  • Dissonance (Offensive) (Level 30): Emits a wave of chaotic sound, dealing 450 Magical DMG and applying the Deafened status effect for 2 turns. (Costs 130 MP)
  • Cacophony (Support) (Level 35): Boosts the user's Magical STR by 40% and increases their spell casting speed by 20% for 3 turns. (Costs 165 MP)
  • Cataclysm (Offensive) (Level 40): Summons a massive chaos-infused boulder that crashes down on a target, dealing 600 Magical DMG. (Costs 270 MP)
  • Disintegration (Offensive) (Level 60): Unleashes a barrage of chaotic energy blasts, dealing 400 Magical DMG to up to 3 targets over 3 turns. (Costs 280 MP)
  • Anarchy's Grasp (Support) (Level 70): Binds a target, preventing them from acting and dealing 200 Magical DMG each turn for 5 turns. (Costs 290 MP)
  • Chaos Storm (Offensive) (Level 75): Summons a storm of chaotic energy that deals 350 Magical DMG to all enemies in a wide area for 3 turns. (Costs 300 MP)
  • Entropy's Embrace (Offensive) (Level 85): Deals 1200 Magical DMG to a single target and has a 60% chance to instantly kill them if they are below 30% HP. (Costs 410 MP)
  • Pandemonium's Grasp (Support) (Level 90): Curses all enemies, forcing them to attack each other randomly for 3 turns. (Costs 550 MP)
  • Anarchy's Wrath (Ultimate) (Level 100): Unleashes a massive wave of chaotic energy, dealing 2000 Magical DMG to all enemies and applying the Cursed status effect for 15 turns. (Costs all remaining MP)
