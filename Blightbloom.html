Blightbloom




Stats:


HP: 900

MP: 0

PHY STR: 14

PHY DEF: 15

MG STR: 3

MG DEF: 13













Blightbloom Skills:

  • Blight Bolt (Offensive) (Level 0): Deals 300 DMG to a single target.
  • Blight Purge (Support) (Level 5): Removes a random positive status effect from the Blightbloom (Can only be used twice per battle).
  • Toxic Spore (Offensive) (Level 15): Deals 450 DMG to a single target and has a 60% chance to apply a Low-Tier Poisoned status effect for 7 turns.
  • Blight Shield (Support) (Level 20): Increases the Blightbloom's PHY DEF by 40% for 5 turns. While active, the Blightbloom gains a 10% damage boost against enemies with positive status effects.
  • Entangle (Offensive) (Level 30): Deals 500 DMG to a single target and ensnares them with decaying vines, rooting them in place for 2 turns (Turn skip effect only works once per battle).
  • Blight Surge (Support) (Level 35): Removes all positive status effects from a single ally and grants them a 30% damage boost for 5 turns. (Can only be used once per battle)
  • Acidic Blight (Offensive) (Level 40): Deals 650 DMG to all enemies in a large area and applies Mid-Tier Poisoned status effect for 5 turns.
  • Blight Bomb (Offensive) (Level 50): Deals 800 DMG to a single target and has a 40% chance to apply the Mid-Tier Stunned status effect for 3 turns, preventing the target from acting.
  • Blight Armor (Support) (Level 55): Increases the Blightbloom's MG DEF by 50% for 5 turns. While active, the Blightbloom gains a 15% damage boost. (Can only be used once per battle)
  • Absorb Element (Support) (Level 60): For 3 turns, the Blightbloom gains a 40% damage boost against a random element type and absorbs 8% of the damage dealt as HP and MP (Can only be used twice per battle).
  • Withering Touch (Offensive) (Level 65): Deals 750 DMG to a single target and has a 50% chance to reduce their PHY ATK and MAG ATK by 20% for 5 turns (effects cannot stack).
  • Blight Nova (Offensive) (Level 70): Deals 900 DMG to all enemies in a large area and has a 30% chance to apply the Cursed status effect for 7 turns.
  • Blight Metamorphosis (Support) (Level 75): Transforms the Blightbloom into a more powerful, corrupted form for 5 turns. In this form, the Blightbloom gains a 30% boost to all stats and a 20% damage boost (Can only be used once per battle).
  • Oblivion's Embrace (Offensive) (Level 100): Deals 1200 DMG to all enemies and applies the Black Heart status effect for 10 turns (Can only be used once per battle).
