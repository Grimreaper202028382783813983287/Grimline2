Zombies















Vampire Racial Traits:

  • Disease Immunity
  • Instant Death Immunity
  • Doubled Damage From Fire
  • Hunger Immunity
  • Life Drain Immunity
  • Holy and Light Vulnerability: All damage taken from attacks related to the Holy and Light element are increased by 20%.
  • Darkness Resistance: All damage taken from attacks related to the Dark element are decreased by 50%
  • Weapon Resistance (Passive): Decreases damage from all unenchanted weapons by 25%.
