Chilled




"The Chilled status effect makes any Water and Ice damage the target takes be increased by 50%. However, it also reduces any Fire damage taken by 50%. If the target receives the Wet status effect while under the Chilled effect, it will immediately be changed to the Frozen status effect instead. Additionally, the Chilled effect will also immediately remove any existing Burn, Scorch, and Berserk status effects from the user."
