Insectoids















Insectoids Racial Traits:

  • Keen Senses (Passive): When battling enemies under the Invisible status effect, insectoids gain a 10% increase to their MG DEF stat.
  • Colony Instincts (Passive): Insectoids gain a 10% increase to their Physical Attack when fighting alongside other insectoid creatures including summons.
  • Metabolic Overdrive (Passive): Insectoids heal 5% of their maximum HP each turn, reflecting their incredible metabolic rate and ability to process energy efficiently. This also grants a 5% increase in the effects of any Regen status effect that they may be under the influence of.
  • Pheromone Shield (Active): Once per battle, the insect can release a powerful pheromone cloud that lasts for 3 turns and deals 350 damage per turn to all enemies. The effect also applies the Poisoned status effect.
  • Emergence (Active): Once per battle, when an insectoid's HP falls below 30%, they can shed their old exoskeleton and emerge stronger, granting a 20% increase to all stats for the duration of the encounter.
  • Hivemind's Fury (Passive): When an insectoid player's summon falls in battle, all nearby insectoids gain a 15% increase to their Physical Attack Power. This ability can stack up to 2 times.
  • Pyrophobic Vulnerability: All damage taken from Fire element attacks is increased by 30% for insectoids.
  • Aquatic Resistance: Damage taken from Water element attacks is reduced by 20%.
  • Terrestrial Adaptation: Damage taken from Earth element attacks is reduced by 20%.
  • Electrophobic Vulnerability: All damage taken from Lightning element attacks is increased by 35%.
