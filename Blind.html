Blind




"The Blind status effect causes the target to suffer from impaired vision and reduced accuracy with their attacks."

Low-Tier: The target has a 70% chance of missing their attacks.

Mid-Tier: The target has a 50% chance of missing their attacks.

High-Tier: The target has a 33% chance of missing their attacks.
