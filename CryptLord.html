Crypt Lord




Stats:


HP: 650

MP: 25

PHY STR: 7

PHY DEF: 15

MG STR: 20

MG DEF: 15













Crypt Lord Spells:

  • Dark Control (Passive): Allows the user to control undead outside of battle, and even send them to other locations to engage in battle with monsters and enemy players (Does not affect bosses).
  • Dark Bullet (Offensive) (Level 0): 150 DMG (Costs 1 MP to use)
  • Dark Shot (Offensive) (Level 5): 250 DMG (Costs 5 MP to use)
  • Raise Dead (Support) (Level 15): Summons a skeleton with the following stats (Costs 15 MP to use):
    • HP: 1850
    • Skills:
      • Dark Slash: 200 DMG
  • Dark Aura (Support) (Level 25): Deals 400 DMG to all foes present and heals all the user’s summoned undead’s HP by 15%. (Costs 35 MP)
  • Necrotic Blast (Offensive) (Level 35): Deals 600 DMG (Costs 45 MP)
  • Soul Steal (Offensive) (Level 45): Deals 400 DMG and heals 12% of the user’s max HP (Costs 50 MP)
  • Dark Pact (Offensive) (Level 55): Increases the DMG of all the skills of one undead summon by 20% for 3 turns. (Costs 65 MP)
  • Undead Horde (Support) (Level 65): Summons 3 Skeletal Warriors with the following stats (Costs 85 MP to use):
    • HP: 2800
    • Skills:
      • Dark Guard (Passive): All foes in the battle must attack the Skeletal Warriors before attacking the player.
      • Dark Cutter: 600 DMG
  • Unholy Ground (Support) (Level 75): Removes the Holy and Light vulnerabilities of everyone present in the battle for 3 turns. (Costs 115 MP to use).
  • Blood Sacrifice (Support) (Level 85): Sacrifices 10% of the user’s HP in order to revive all of their undead back from the dead. (Costs 135 MP to use)
  • Summon: Death Lord (Support) (Level 95): Summons forth a Death Lord with the following stats (Costs all available MP to use):
    • HP:4000
    • MG DEF: 120
    • Elemental Resistance: Decreases all Lightning, Ice, and Earth elemental damage by 50%.
    • Skills:
      • Dark Eclipse: Deals 1100 DMG to all foes and restores 50% of the max HP of all undead present Can only be used once every three turns (Bypassess all counter-skills and counter-spells that would remove, evade, or negate the DMG from this attack, though they can still be activated).
      • Dark Mist: Increases the DMG of all undead present by 35% and removes their Light and Holy vulnerability so long as the Death Lord remains alive.
