Plant Monsters















Plant Monster Racial Traits:

  • Photosynthesis (Passive): Plant Monsters gain a 10% HP boost each turn when in daylight.
  • Rooted (Passive): When a Plant Monster's HP falls below 50%, their PHY DEF and MG DEF increase by 20%.
  • Nature's Blessing (Passive): Plant Monsters have an immunity to poison.
  • Earth's Embrace (Passive): Plant Monsters have a 15% resistance to Earth element attacks.
  • Fire Vulnerability (Passive): Plant Monsters take a 30% damage increase from Fire element attacks.
  • Lightning Vulnerability (Passive): Plant Monsters take a 25% damage increase from Electric element attacks.
