Shifters







Stats:


HP: 530

MP: 0

PHY STR: 5

PHY DEF: 5

MG STR: 5

MG DEF: 5

Alignement: Neutral












Shifters Racial Traits:

  • Armor Breaker (Passive): User cannot equip armor or weapons.
  • Fresh Start (Passive): User will automatically be transported to a place where a player is located at.
  • Mimic's Resilience (Passive): Shifters gain a 20% resistance to all status effects.
  • Mimic's Fortitude (Passive): Shifters are immune to the following status effects: Hunger, Tired, and Dry.
  • Metamorphic Regeneration (Passive): Once per battle, when a Shifter's HP falls below 30%, they automatically transform into their most recently used alternate form (Except the one that they’re in currently). This transformation grants them a 30% damage reduction for the next turn and heals them for 20% of their maximum HP.
  • Empathic Adaptation (Passive): When a Shifter transforms into another player, they gain a 15% boost to the transformed player's highest stat for that form. This bonus persists until the Shifter transforms again.
  • Mimic's Tenacity (Passive): Shifters are immune to the Instant Death, Auto-Revive, Brave, and Defended status effects.
  • Fire Vulnerability: Shifters take 20% increased damage from Fire element attacks.
  • Water Vulnerability: Shifters take a 10% damage increase from Water element attacks.
  • Earth Vulnerability: Shifters take a 15% damage increase from Earth element attacks.
  • Ice Vulnerability: Shifters take a 15% damage increase from Ice element attacks.
  • Nature Vulnerability: Shifters take a 10% damage increase from Nature element attacks.
  • Light Vulnerability: Shifters take a 60% increased damage from Light element attacks.
  • Darkness Resistance: Darkness element attacks are reduced by 50%.
  • Wind Resistance: Wing element attacks are reduced by 35%.
  • Lightning Vulnerability: Shifters take a 25% damage increase from Lightning element attacks.
  • Chaos Vulnerability: Shifters take a 70% damage increase from Chaos element attacks.


Shifter Skills:

  • Metamorphosis (Support) (Level 0): The Shifter transforms into a random player within the same location as them, gaining access to that player's abilities and skills. The Shifter's original stats, passive skills, resistances, and vulnerabilities remain unchanged. This transformation can last for 5 of the user’s turns (Outside of battle) or until the Shifter uses another skill. (Can only be used once per battle while inside battle) (Can be used outside of battle).
  • Adaptive Strikes (Support) (Level 15): The Shifter's next attack has a 25% chance to inflict a random status effect based on the enemy's race or class. Available effects include: Blind, Chilled, Cursed, Enraged, or Poisoned. (Can be used twice per battle)
  • Metamorphic Leap (Support) (Level 30) The Shifter leaps to a targeted location, dealing 200 DMG to enemies in that area, and immediately engaging into combat with said enemies. They also gain a 20% boost to a random stat for 2 turns. (Can be used outside of battle)
  • Chameleonic Camouflage (Support) (Level 65): The Shifter transforms into a race that blends in with their surroundings, becoming invisible in natural environments for 3 turns. While camouflaged, the Shifter gains a 30% damage reduction and a 15% boost to a random stat. Enemies attacking the Shifter during this time have a 20% chance to attack an empty space, wasting their turn. (Can be used once per battle)
