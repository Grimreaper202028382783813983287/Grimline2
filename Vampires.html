Vampire Racial Traits:

  • Disease Immunity
  • Instant Death Immunity
  • Doubled Damage From Fire
  • Hunger Immunity
  • Life Drain Immunity
  • Holy and Light Vulnerability: All damage taken from attacks related to the Holy and Light element are increased by 40%.
  • Darkness Resistance: All damage taken from attacks related to the Dark element are decreased by 30%
  • Life Absorption (Offensive): Deals 500 DMG, and causes the user to gain HP equal to 10% of the targetâ€™s current HP (Can only be used 3 times per battle).
  • Fast Regeneration (Passive): Causes the Player to gain HP equal to 10% of their maximum HP each turn.
  • Weapon Resistance (Passive): Decreases damage from all unenchanted weapons by 15%.
