Morale




"The Morale status effect allows those with it to endure an attack that would kill them, leaving them at 1 HP (Does not work a second time). Cancels out the Cursed status effect, and prevents Instant Death for a certain amount of time."

Low-Tier: Lasts for two turns.

Mid-Tier: Lasts for three turns.

High-Tier: Lasts for five turns.
