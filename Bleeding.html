Bleeding




“When a target is afflicted with the Bleeding status effect, blood vessels rupture beneath their skin, causing them to lose a significant amount of blood each turn. The amount of damage dealt by the Bleeding effect increases with each tier, reflecting the severity of the wound.”

Low-Tier Bleeding: Decreases the target's PHY DEF stat by 20%. Deals damage equal to 8% of the target's current HP each turn (Lasts for 3 turns).

Mid-Tier Bleeding: Decreases the target's PHY DEF stat by 30%. Deals damage equal to 12% of the target's current HP each turn (Lasts for 5 turns).

High-Tier Bleeding: Decreases the target's PHY DEF stat by 50%. Deals damage equal to 18% of the target's current HP each turn (Lasts for 7 turns).
