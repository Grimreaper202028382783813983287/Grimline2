Haste




"The Haste status effect enables the user to skip a certain number of turns in battle, making all status effects and special effects occur sooner."

Low-Tier: The effect skips one turn in battle.

Mid-Tier: The effect skips two turns in battle.

High-Tier: The effect skips three turns in battle.
