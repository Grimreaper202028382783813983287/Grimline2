Currency




Currency: Currency is a mechanic in Grimline that allows players to purchase various items in exchange for a certain amount of coins.









List of Currency Types:







Copper: Copper is the lowest form of currency in all of Grimline. It is normally used to purchase low-level items and weapons.






Silver: Silver is another form of currency Grimline, ranking slightly above Copper. Each piece of Silver is worth 10 pieces of Copper. It is often used for the purchase of mid-level items and weapons.



Gold: Gold is the highest form of currency in Grimline. Each piece of Gold is worth 50 pieces of Silver, as well as 500 pieces of copper. It is often used for the purchase of high-level items and weapons.
