Demi-Human




Stats:


HP: 960

MP: 0

PHY STR: 20

PHY DEF: 18

MG STR: 5

MG DEF: 12













Demi-Human Skills:

  • Primal Claws (Offensive) (Level 0): The Demi-Human unleashes its claws in a vicious swipe, dealing 150 DMG to a single target and having a 25% chance to apply the Bleeding status effect for 3 turns.
  • Pack Tackle (Offensive) (Level 5): The Demi-Human lunges at a single target, dealing 300 DMG. If the target is hit, the user and their party gain a 15% boost to PHY ATK for 3 turns (Can only be used twice per battle).
  • Bestial Rage (Support) (Level 10): Enrages the Demi-Human, increasing their PHY ATK by 30% and their PHY DEF by 20% for 4 turns. While enraged, the Demi-Human's skills have a 15% chance to deal an additional 400 DMG. (Can only be used once per battle).
  • Claw Strike (Offensive) (Level 15) - The Demi-Human strikes with incredible speed and precision, dealing 200 DMG to a single target and having a 40% chance to apply a Low-Tier Stunned status effect (Can only be used twice per battle).
  • Pack Leader's Call (Support) (Level 20): The Demi-Human lets out a mighty roar, granting all party members a 20% boost to PHY ATK and a 15% boost to PHY DEF for 5 turns (Can only be used twice per battle).
  • Feral Pounce (Offensive) (Level 25): The Demi-Human leaps at a single target, dealing 750 DMG and having a 30% chance to apply a Low-Tier Stunned status effect (Can only be used once per battle).
  • Beastial Frenzy (Offensive) (Level 35): The Demi-Human enters a state of primal, unbridled rage, increasing their PHY ATK by 40% for 3 turns. While in Frenzy, the Demi-Human's skills have a 20% chance to deal an additional 500 DMG (Can only be used once per battle).
  • Alpha's Wrath (Offensive) (Level 45): The Demi-Human unleashes a mighty roar, dealing 500 DMG to all enemies and having a 60% chance to apply the Misfortune status effect for 2 turns.
  • Pack Alpha (Support) (Level 50): If the Demi-Human is the last party member standing, they gain a 30% boost to all stats and a 20% boost to damage dealt for the remainder of the battle (Can only be used once per battle).
  • Pack Mentality (Counter) (Level 60): When the user falls in battle, they resurrect once with 50% of their maximum HP and MP if another Demi-Human is nearby (Can only be used once per battle).
  • Primal Savagery (Offensive) (Level 65): The Demi-Human channels their inner beast, increasing their PHY ATK by 50% for 2 turns (Can only be used three times per battle).
  • Alpha's Judgment (Judgment) (Level 70): The Demi-Human passes judgment on a single target, dealing 700 DMG and applying a random negative status effect.
  • Pack Alpha's Blessing (Support) (Level 75): The Demi-Human blesses the party, granting all members a 30% boost to PHY ATK and a 20% boost to PHY DEF for 5 turns (Can only be used three times per battle).
  • Beastial Onslaught (Support) (Level 80) - The Demi-Human and their party gain a 40% boost to PHY ATK and a 20% boost to movement speed for 4 turns. Enemies hit by a Demi-Human's attack during this time take 150 DMG per turn and have a 20% chance to suffer the Bleed status effect for 2 turns (Can only be used twice per battle).
  • Pack Alpha's Fury (Support) (Level 90) - The Demi-Human enters a state of unbridled, primal fury, increasing all physical stats to max for 3 turns, and restoring half of their max HP (Can only be used once per battle).
  • Primal Rage (Offensive) (Level 100) - The Demi-Human taps into the raw, untamed power of their inner beast, dealing 1100 DMG to all enemies and applying a random negative status effect for 5 turns (Can only be used once per battle).
