Skeletons















Skeletons Racial Traits:

  • Disease Immunity
  • Instant Death Immunity
  • Doubled Damage From Fire
  • Ice Resistance: Decreases all ice DMG taken by 25%
  • Hunger Immunity
  • Life Drain Immunity
  • Holy and Light Vulnerability: All damage taken from attacks related to the Holy and Light element are increased by 15%.
  • Darkness Resistance: All damage taken from attacks related to the Dark element are decreased by 65%
  • Weapon Resistance (Passive): Decreases damage from all unenchanted weapons by 35%.
