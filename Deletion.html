Deletion




"Deletion causes non-elemental damage equal to 1.5% of the target's maximum HP each turn while the effect is active. This status effect cannot be removed except by items and skills that specifically counter it. Furthermore, the Deletion status effect prevents the target from fleeing, trapping them in the battle."
