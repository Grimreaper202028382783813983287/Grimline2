Wraith




Stats:


HP: 550

MP: 15

PHY STR: 0

PHY DEF: 0

MG STR: 10

MG DEF: 5













Wraith Spells:

  • Phantom Strike (Offensive) (Level 0): Deals 250 DMG to a single target. (Costs 15 MP)
  • Emotion Leech (Support) (Level 10): Drains 100 HP from the target and steals a random positive status effect from a target, granting it to the user for 5 turns. (Costs 20 MP)
  • Shadow Dash (Support) (Level 25): Allows the Wraith to teleport a short distance, leaving behind a shadowy decoy that takes 50% of the Wraith's DMG for 2 turns. (Costs 25 MP)
  • Nightfall (Offensive) (Level 45): Summons a wave of darkness that deals 750 DMG to all enemies in a cone, with increased damage to enemies with the Cursed status effect. (Costs 50 MP)
  • Mass Confusion (Support) (Level 60): Selects up to 2 targets and causes them to attack each other randomly for 5 turns. (Can only be used if there are at least two foes present) (Costs 65 MP)
  • Unholy Shield (Support) ( Level 70): Grants the user a shield that absorbs up to 2000 DMG. Lasts 5 turns. (Costs 75 MP)
  • Curse of the Wraith (Support) (Level 80): Curses a target, causing them to deal 20% less DMG to the user and take 20% increased DMG from enemies for 7 turns. (Costs 85 MP)
  • Shadow's Grasp (Offensive) (Level 90): Pulls a single target towards the user and deals 900 DMG, with a 50% chance to apply the Cursed status effect for 3 turns. (Costs 95 MP)
  • Abyssal Form (Support) (Level 100): Transforms the Wraith into an Abyssal entity, gaining immunity to magic damage for 3 turns, as well as a 25% increase in DMG on all attacks. (Costs all remaining MP)
